About Me!

Hello! I am Mushfiq. Currently, I am majoring in computer science. But before sketching a mental image of a guy behind a blue screen typing in codes, I would like to challenge that image. Ideas that might show a newer perspective. I hope, a rather artistic one.

Maybe I am a detective in Gotham city trying to figure out batman's identity on other days. But instead of going after clues, I implement logic in a slightly different way to solve a piece of a grand puzzle. Then almost immediately jump to another.
But it is not always like this. Somedays, I feel stuck, in a deadlock. But eventually, I find my way back. Or fight my way back.
